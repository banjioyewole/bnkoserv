Frunk Me Daddy Privacy Policy

Last Updated July 20, 2020

Frunk Me Daddy handles sensitive data surrounding your Tesla account. The following statements govern how we use your data to make our apps better.

Simply stated, Frunk Me Daddy doesn't retain any sensitive information you provide, Just the token retunred to us after accessing Tesla's API on your behalf. This token is required to make requests on your behalf. We also retain your selected vehicle to send requests to. Both the token and the vehicle name are stored locally on your device.

Additionally, Frunk Me Daddy doesn't have any analytics trackers, or in-app advertisements.
