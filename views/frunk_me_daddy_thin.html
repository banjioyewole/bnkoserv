FRUNK ME DADDY
The fastest Frunk opener in the West.

"Frunk" - Front Trunk.

You know how in the movies, the protagonist never looks back at the explosion? I wanted the same thing, except that it's when I'm walking towards... my ca– What I'm trying to say is that I wanted to be able to open my frunk before I finished walking to my car. Tesla's app, while fantastic at a hundred different things, is not the fastest way to open the frunk. So I implemented my own.

Frunk Me Daddy solves the speed problem by having access to the frunk button from a home screen shortcut. And on devices with Force Touch, it's about 50% faster than the Tesla App. (Preformed slowly here so you can see what's happening)
