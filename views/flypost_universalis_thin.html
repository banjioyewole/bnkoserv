FLYPOST UNIVERSALIS
Turn student engagement up to 11.

Flypost is a university-centric event marketing platform. We wanted to create a place for everything Puget Sound and designed a solution. We believe Flypost to be a forward-thinking approach to handling marketing and viewing schedules that is leagues more sustainable than the current paper-based practice.

We wanted to build a platform that is easy to navigate and puts user content first. With these considerations in mind, we built out a platform that makes it easier than ever to find information about what's going on right now or weeks from now, all from the palm of your hand.
