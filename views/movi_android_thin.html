MOVI CINEMA ROBOT
Cinematic moves, Robotic control.

Movi is a handheld gimbal that enables people to create smooth cinematic movies all with the power of their smartphone. In six months I built an Android app capable of recording 4K video and talking to Movi and achieved feature parity with the iOS app.

The camera part of the app uses the latest APIs to enable high FPS and high-bitrate recording rivaling DSLRs and camcorders. The app also enables Movi methods like Movilapse, a specialized recording mode allows for Timelapse speed videos to take you through space too.
