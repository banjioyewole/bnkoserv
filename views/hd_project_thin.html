THE HD PROJECT
The purest expression of the HD Project.

The HD Project was one of the first things I ever did as a new designer and developer. It started as a place to store pictures I've taken, and quickly set them as my wallpaper. Over time I continued to update the application moving images from being embedded in the app to being hosted on this very server.

My most recent update continues to provide access to my favorite pictures and makes it easier than ever to add new content from anywhere. Its current iteration stands above the others in that it's even more lightweight, robust and easier to use than ever.
